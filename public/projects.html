My Projects

  • Alzaware: ML Model for early Alzheimer’s diagnosis and risk prediction:
    Alzaware is an AI-powered medical project focused on the early detection of Alzheimer’s disease using MRI scans and machine learning. Developed an AI/ML model that analyzes MRI brain scans to predict early-stage Alzheimer’s disease with high reliability. Built the complete preprocessing and CNN based detection pipeline, integrating lifestyle data insights for improved diagnostic accuracy

  • Alzaware EEG: Analyzing EEG signals to detect cognitive decline
    Led the development of Alzaware EEG is an AI-driven module that analyzes EEG signals to detect early cognitive decline and assess Alzheimer’s risk. Developed initial ML models to classify EEG patterns associated with memory impairment, integrating them with the existing Alzaware ecosystem. Coordinated research, dataset design, and architecture decisions, ensuring the project aligned with medical AI best practices and long-term scalability.
